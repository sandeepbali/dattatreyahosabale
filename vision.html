Ideology & Thought

Vision for a transformed society

The Five-Fold Path

Panch Parivartan

Five dimensions of societal transformation envisioned under Hosabale's leadership — a holistic framework for national renewal.

01
Samarasta
समरसता — Social Harmony

Equality with fraternity. Strengthening voices against discrimination, eradicating untouchability, building social harmony through unity.

02
Family Awakening
कुटुम्ब प्रबोधन

Strengthening the family unit as the foundation of a healthy, resilient society. Promoting familial values and bonds across generations.

03
Ecological Living
पर्यावरण संरक्षण

Promoting an environment-friendly lifestyle rooted in the Indian tradition of reverence for nature and all living beings.

04
Swadeshi Conduct
स्वदेशी आचरण — Sense of Self

Inculcating selfhood based on Bharatiya values. Integrating traditions with modernity without losing national identity.

05
Civic Duty
नागरिक कर्तव्य बोध

Social awakening for civic responsibility. Every citizen must fulfill their duty to vote — striving for 100% voter participation.

Core Beliefs

Key philosophical positions

On Nation & Rashtra

"India is an ancient rashtra, distinct from the Western notion of a nation, with Sanskrit as its foundation."

On Social Unity

"Caste has no place in the Sangh." Hindu unity as a larger unifying identity transcending divisive lines.

On Development

Development should encompass spiritual and mental advancement — self-sufficient villages not solely dependent on government.

On Societal Harmony

"While ideological opposition is natural, there should not be personal hatred in society."

On Cultural Awakening

Changing the intellectual narrative from a national perspective, mobilizing "sajjan shakti" for social change.

On the Sangh's Journey

"From neglect and ridicule to curiosity and acceptance" — driven by Dr. Hedgewar's vision.

Every citizen must think about their contribution to enhancing the nation's dignity and dedicate time for this cause.
— Dattatreya Hosabale

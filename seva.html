Nepal 2015

Leading relief beyond borders

During the devastating Nepal earthquake in April 2015, Dattatreya Hosabale personally headed the relief team and material dispatch organized by the Sangh — demonstrating that seva knows no boundaries.

The work of the Sangh is precisely for societal benefit, with no special agenda beyond the progress of the people.
— Dattatreya Hosabale

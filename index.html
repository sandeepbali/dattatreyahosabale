The Sarkaryavah

Leading the world's largest voluntary organization

Elected Sarkaryavah on March 20, 2021 — succeeding Suresh 'Bhaiyyaji' Joshi — Dattatreya Hosabale is the executive head of the RSS. The second Kannadiga to hold this position. Re-elected in 2024 for the 2024–2027 term.

Career Milestones

Five decades of dedicated service

1954
Born in Hosabale Village
Born December 1st in Sorab Taluk, Shimoga, Karnataka. A family steeped in RSS tradition and selfless service.
Pivotal Moment
1968
Joins the RSS
At 13, formally joins the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh. The daily Shakha becomes his crucible of discipline.
1972
Joins ABVP
Becomes member of Akhil Bharatiya Vidyarthi Parishad while at National College, Bengaluru.
Trial by Fire
1975–77
Imprisoned During the Emergency
Detained 14–16 months under MISA for opposing Indira Gandhi's Emergency. Among thousands of RSS workers who chose jail over submission.
1978
Full-time Pracharak, ABVP
Becomes full-time worker, forsaking personal career for the cause of voluntary service.
Major Role
1992
National Organizing Secretary, ABVP
Appointed Sangathan Mantri at Kanpur session. Holds this for 11 years as ABVP grows into world's largest student organization.
2004
Returns to RSS — Sah-Bauddhik Pramukh
After two decades transforming ABVP, returns to RSS core as joint-in-charge of intellectual activities.
2009
Sah-Sarkaryavah (Joint General Secretary)
Elevated to key decision-making role. Serves for 12 years until 2021.
2015
Nepal Earthquake Relief
Personally heads relief team and material dispatch during the devastating Nepal earthquake.
Historic Appointment
2021
Becomes Sarkaryavah of RSS
Elected General Secretary on March 20, 2021, succeeding Suresh 'Bhaiyyaji' Joshi. The second Kannadiga to hold this position.
Reaffirmed
2024
Re-elected Sarkaryavah
Re-elected for the 2024–2027 term. Stewards the RSS towards its historic centenary.
